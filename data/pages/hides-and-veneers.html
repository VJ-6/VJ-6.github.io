HIDES

Every inch of the interior of the Continental GT is upholstered by hand. Crafting the hides is a time-honoured process that involves skills that take years to learn. From the seats to the fascia and the steering wheel, every part of this extraordinary car requires a different approach. Which is why sections of the hide with high natural elasticity are used for curved surfaces like the seats, rather than for piping or flatter areas such as door inserts.
