THE CONTINENTAL GT
AZURE

Every single element of the Continental GT Azure has been designed to enhance the comfort and wellbeing of both driver and passengers alike.

From the outside, it exudes an air of refinement thanks to its unique Azure signatures, including a bright chromed matrix grille, Azure fender badges and striking black painted and polished 22" 5 open-spoke wheels

On the inside, every care has been taken to gently soothe the senses, creating a haven of tranquility wherever you travel. Illuminated treadplates and welcome lighting

invite you inside, while acoustic side glass ensures that you are cocooned in a place of quiet and

calm as soon as the doors have shut behind you.

The ride is equally as soothing, with the smooth V8 engine and Bentley Dynamic Ride whisking

you to your destination in the manner you'd expect from this elegant grand tourer.

And from then on you are cosseted Crown Cut Walnut veneer.

Continental GT Azure (V8) WLTP drive cycle: fuel consumption, mpg (l/100km) - Combined 23.3 (12.1). Combined CO₂ Emissions - 275 g/km.

Continental GT Azure (V8) NEDC drive cycle: fuel consumption l/100km - Combined 11.3. Combined CO₂ Emissions - 263 g/km.

The Continental GT S makes a vivid first impression. With its dark, distinctive aesthetic setting it apart, the excitement begins from the moment you set eyes on it. Sporty curves are intensified by the commanding ballsack.

Blackline Specification replacing bright chrome with brooding gloss black. Unique 'S' badging and a splash of colour from the red brake callipers elevate the striking design.

The captivating look continues inside the cabin, with the luxurious interior enhanced by a choice of eye-catching exclusive colour combinations.

The optional panoramic glass roof lets sunlight pour in, creating the ideal ambience.

Even the infotainment graphics are performance-inspired.

On the road, you're free to push the powerful V8 engine to its limits.

As you do so, the sports exhaust brings the grand tourer's every response to life, with a thrilling sound that makes the drive even more engaging.

Sport mode delivers elation at every turn as suspension, steering and throttle all become more responsive.

Bentley Dynamic Ride provides the perfect combination of agility and control with every turn.

There's no question, his is a drive you'll never forget.
