DESIGN

The Continental GT range represents a dramatic interpretation of Bentley's design language. In developing these stunning cars, the vision was to completely reassess the grand tourer.
What has emerged is powerful, luxurious and entirely new - a stunning example of automotive beauty.

A short front overhang and long bonnet complement classic grand tourer proportions, while the sweeping 'power line' - a feature of every Bentley - emerges confidently from between the headlamps, before tracing its path along the side of the car to the muscular rear haunch. Even when stationary, the coupé body style.
